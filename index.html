About Me

Hello, I'm Achmad Prihartanto. For over 20 years, including 14 in the high-precision environment of Siemens, I've managed the financial engine of technology-driven companies. My foundation is a Master's degree in Finance, and my framework is the strategic discipline of a Certified Management Accountant (CMA).

I view finance not as a back-office function, but as a company's strategic co-pilot. My expertise lies in building the bridge between marketing spend and financial results. I don't just report on the budget; I partner with marketing and operations teams to answer the most critical question: "Are we acquiring customers profitably?"

I specialize in:

  • Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A): Acting as a strategic business partner to leadership, translating complex data into actionable insights for long-range planning, investment decisions, and capital allocation.
  • Marketing & Sales Funnel ROI Analysis: Optimizing go-to-market spend by building financial models that connect marketing investments directly to profitable customer acquisition and lifetime value (CLV).
  • Operational Excellence: Streamlining processes to improve efficiency and protect margins, a discipline honed at Siemens.

I am currently seeking a senior leadership role where I can apply my unique blend of financial rigor and marketing acumen to help a technology-focused company scale profitably. Let's connect to discuss how I can bring financial clarity and strategic direction to your growth objectives.
